# Product Mission (Lite)

BookStock is a comprehensive inventory management system that helps publishing companies eliminate spreadsheet chaos by providing intelligent stock tracking, automated royalty calculations, and real-time analytics across multiple warehouses.

BookStock serves publishing companies managing 200+ book titles across multiple warehouses, replacing manual spreadsheet workflows with automated inventory tracking and financial calculations. Unlike generic inventory systems, BookStock provides publishing-specific intelligence including ISBN tracking, automated royalty calculations based on RRP percentages and sales thresholds, and intelligent reprint triggers based on sales velocity and configurable stock levels.
