# Product Mission

## Pitch

BookStock is a comprehensive inventory management system that helps publishing companies eliminate spreadsheet chaos by providing intelligent stock tracking, automated royalty calculations, and real-time analytics across multiple warehouses.

## Users

### Primary Customers

- **Small to Medium Publishing Houses**: Companies managing 200+ book titles across multiple warehouses who need to replace manual spreadsheet workflows with automated systems
- **Publishing Operations Teams**: Staff responsible for inventory management, sales tracking, and financial reporting who require real-time visibility into stock levels and profitability

### User Personas

**Publishing Operations Manager** (35-50 years old)
- **Role:** Head of Operations
- **Context:** Manages inventory across UK, US, and online fulfillment warehouses for 200+ active titles
- **Pain Points:** Manual spreadsheet updates, lack of real-time stock visibility, complex royalty calculations, missed reprint opportunities
- **Goals:** Automate inventory tracking, reduce manual errors, optimize warehouse allocation, ensure adequate stock levels

**Financial Controller** (40-55 years old)
- **Role:** Finance Director
- **Context:** Responsible for profitability analysis and royalty payments across entire catalog
- **Pain Points:** Time-consuming profit calculations, manual royalty computations, delayed financial reporting
- **Goals:** Automated profit tracking, accurate royalty calculations, real-time financial insights

## The Problem

### Manual Inventory Chaos

Publishing companies rely on complex spreadsheets to track 200+ titles across multiple warehouses, leading to errors, delays, and missed opportunities. This manual process costs approximately 20 hours per week in administrative overhead and results in stockouts that can lose 15-30% of potential sales.

**Our Solution:** Automated inventory tracking with real-time synchronization across all warehouses and sales channels.

### Complex Financial Calculations

Publishers struggle with manual profit and royalty calculations involving RRP, unit costs, sales thresholds, and percentage-based payments. These calculations are error-prone and time-consuming, often delaying author payments by weeks.

**Our Solution:** Automated profit margin calculations and royalty processing with configurable thresholds and payment schedules.

### Lack of Predictive Intelligence

Without proper analytics, publishers can't predict when to reprint titles, leading to either stockouts or overstock situations. This reactive approach costs publishers an average of Â£50,000 annually in lost sales and excess inventory costs.

**Our Solution:** Intelligent reprint triggers based on sales velocity and configurable stock level thresholds.

## Differentiators

### Publishing-Specific Intelligence

Unlike generic inventory systems, BookStock is built specifically for book publishing workflows with native support for ISBN tracking, series management, and publishing-specific calculations like RRP-based profit margins and royalty structures.

### Multi-Warehouse Optimization

While other systems treat warehouses as separate entities, BookStock provides intelligent allocation recommendations and automated stock movement tracking to optimize inventory across UK, US, and online fulfillment centers.

### Automated Financial Intelligence

BookStock automatically calculates complex publishing financials including profit margins (RRP/2 - unit cost), threshold-based royalties, and sales velocity analytics that generic systems cannot handle without extensive customization.

## Key Features

### Core Features

- **ISBN-Based Inventory Tracking:** Complete catalog management with ISBN, author, format, page count, and RRP tracking
- **Multi-Warehouse Management:** Real-time stock levels across UK, US, and online fulfillment warehouses
- **Automated Stock Movements:** Track printing, reprints, and inter-warehouse transfers
- **Sales & Damage Logging:** Monthly data import from warehouse partners with automated reconciliation
- **Series Management:** Group titles by series (e.g., "Opinionated Guides", "East London Photo Stories") for better organization

### Financial Features

- **Profit Calculation Engine:** Automated profit tracking using RRP/2 minus unit cost formula
- **Royalty Management:** Configurable percentage-based royalties with sales threshold triggers
- **Monthly Financial Reports:** Comprehensive profitability analysis across all titles and time periods
- **Cost Center Tracking:** Unit cost management and profit margin analysis

### Analytics Features

- **Sales Velocity Analysis:** Track monthly, 12-month, year-to-date, and lifetime sales performance
- **Stock Level Monitoring:** Real-time "months of stock remaining" calculations based on sales velocity
- **Reprint Alerts:** Automated triggers when stock levels fall below 3-month thresholds
- **Performance Dashboard:** Executive-level insights into catalog performance and profitability
